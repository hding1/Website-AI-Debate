Poster Two: 30 Years

 Artificial intelligence provides machines’ abilities to “learn” and “solve problems”. In March 2016, AlphaGo, a computer Go program beat a human professional Go player by maximizing the probability of winning through 10^200 choices , which indicates that human are no longer the only ruler of strategy games. Maybe 30 years later, AI can really think and behave like humans can become our friends. Nowadays we play board game with people, but we play with robots after decades of years.

- Xingyu Zhao
